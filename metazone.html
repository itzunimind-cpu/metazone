Why we built it

Indian BGMI is, mechanically, among the best in the world.
Decision-making is not.

Wins are celebrated without understanding why. Losses repeat without a structure to examine them. Coaches track scrims in voice notes. Analysts reconstruct match data from screenshots and Excel sheets that nobody opens twice.

This is not a talent problem. It is an infrastructure problem.

Metazone is the infrastructure. A human watches the match, logs the kill, draws the path, marks the zone. Metazone collects that work and structures it clearly, consistently, in one place. So the next decision is informed by the last hundred.

It isn't an AI insight tool. No algorithm can read the pressure of a zone or the weight of a rotation call. A coach can. Metazone makes sure the coach is looking at the right numbers when they do.
